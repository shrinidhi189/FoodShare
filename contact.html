Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can someone pick up my food donation?

Pickup times vary based on location and availability, but we typically coordinate pickups within 2-4 hours of donation listing. For urgent donations, we prioritize quick matching with nearby recipients.

What types of food can I donate?

We accept a wide variety of foods including prepared meals, fresh produce, packaged goods, and baked items. All donations must be safe for consumption and properly stored. We cannot accept expired items or food that has been temperature abused.

Is there a cost to use FoodShare?

FoodShare is free for both donors and recipients. Our operations are supported through grants, donations, and partnerships. Delivery costs are covered by our partner network or volunteer drivers.

How do I become a delivery partner?

Delivery partners can apply through our website. We work with both established delivery services and volunteer drivers. All partners receive training on safe food handling and our operational procedures.

What areas do you currently serve?

We currently operate in 15 major cities across the country and are expanding regularly. Check our website for the most up-to-date service areas, or contact us to inquire about expansion to your location.
